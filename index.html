RUCEKELI INTERMIXTE Ltd

RUCEKELI INTERMIXTE Ltd is one of the largest company in Rwanda offering premium services and products that are widely used in construction sector,

  • mainly in construction of buildings.
  • roads Bridges.
  • electricity and water facilities.

With a track record spanning over 14 years, we’re known for our diverse and extensive list of operations and products

Founded in 2010, our company is now a fully integrated organization made of six (06) main business streams. Today, we’re known as one of the leading local construction and producer of various construction materials with a reputation for excellence, reliability and innovation. As a result, we are regularly contracted to supply our products and/or services to challenging and/or huge projects across the country.
